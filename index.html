We are open

We are an independently owned and run group of South London based community garden stores. We pride ourselves on our highly knowledgeable team of horticulturalists who are always on hand to offer expert advice regarding all indoor and outdoor gardening matters in a friendly manner. We aim to cater for the needs of all types of urban gardener and home jungle enthusiast. Whether you’re a seasoned landscape artist looking for specialty plants; an enthusiastic novice creating your first garden; or you simply want to brighten up your home or find an exceptional gift for someone special, we guarantee to help.

Welcome to the jungle

Indoor plants can add a touch of natural beauty to any home. As indoor plant specialists, we always aim to have an interesting range in stock, from ‘on trend’ favourites, to some large feature specimens for that instant makeover.

Candles and Diffusers
